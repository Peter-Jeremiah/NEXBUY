Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?
You can track your order status directly in the "Orders" section of your profile. Once shipped, you will also receive a tracking link via email.
What is the return policy?
We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for most items. Items must be in their original condition and packaging.
How do I cancel my order?
Orders can only be cancelled before they are processed for shipping. Please navigate to the order details to attempt a cancellation, or contact support immediately.
